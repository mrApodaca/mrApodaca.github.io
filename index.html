High School Math Curriculum

Please click on the folder above to view the full high school math curriculum.

Math education is much more than just memorizing formulas and trying to remember when it is appropriate to use them. Math education is necessary to help students build logical problem solving skills that all individuals need in a complex global economy. The traditional algebra based system of math education that has been a staple of American education has recently been upgraded to emphasize the importance of conceptual understanding and application of mathematics to real world situations. Students will learn the importance of why components of the algebraic system work and the value of each component is to the system as a whole. Our goal at Centennial is that every student who walks away from here with a diploma understands the value of mathematics and is a capable practitioner of mathematics in their chosen path.

The curriculum that Centennial is currently using is Eureka Math, which is created by created by Common Core, Inc. This curriculum also goes “EngageNY” which are the same exact lessons with a different brand name. After a rigorous review conducted by the Louisiana Department of Education, Eureka Math was considered the only Tier 1 curriculum that meets the demands of the Common Core State Standards for mathematics.
